Our Story

Formed in the vibrant tech hub of Bangalore (Indias Silicon Valley), CodeCraft was born from a simple idea: "What if we could use our coding skills to make a real difference?"

Since then, weve participated in numerous hackathons, coding competitions, and innovation challenges, pushing our limits and learning from every experience. Our journey has taken us from local university competitions to national and international stages, where weve tackled challenges in healthcare, sustainability, education, and smart cities.

Our Mission

We believe technology should empower communities. Whether its optimizing urban logistics, enhancing accessibility, or revolutionizing healthcareour goal is to build tools that make a tangible difference.
